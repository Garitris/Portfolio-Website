VT-Δ /VS070612bl

"A void in fabric, touched by red."

VT-Δ /VS070612bl is an engineered void of warmth and resilience. Constructed from waterproof technical textiles with a matte, shadow-black surface, it resists the elements while shaping a sharp, architectural silhouette. Insulated channels fold around the body like controlled negative space, balancing volume and restraint. Subtle red pull-tabs and precision zippers punctuate the absence, leaving deliberate marks of identity within the monochrome field. Lightweight yet commanding, the vest is designed to layer seamlessly—whether over fabric or skin—transforming motion into presence. More than outerwear, it is a protective shell of void and trace, a fragment of architecture worn.
