VT-Δ /PT004827bl

"A framework of shadow, cut for movement."

VT-Δ /PT004827bl are engineered voids in motion. Constructed from waterproof technical fabrics with a matte, shadow-black finish, they resist the elements while articulating a sculptural silhouette. Angled seams and paneling structure the leg like architectural supports, balancing strength with flow. Concealed drawcords and adaptive closures transform the fit, shifting from tapered precision to relaxed utility. Red accent tags and zipper pulls mark identity within absence, subtle traces of presence in the field of black. More than apparel, these pants are a designed framework: absence made wearable, motion structured into form.
