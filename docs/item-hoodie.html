VT-Δ /HD05917bl

"A void in fabric, bound by red."

The VT-Δ /HD05917bl is a sculptural void in motion. Crafted from waterproof technical fabrics with watertight pockets and precision YKK zippers, it merges utility with presence. A large, stealthy hood envelopes you, while adjustable cables cinch the silhouette to shape space as you move. Black dominates, a canvas interrupted only by subtle red accent tags, zippers, and micro-fabric details—marking the trace of identity within absence. Every contour, every surface change, is deliberate. Attention between structure and flow, shadow and light. This is more than a garment. It is a statement in absence, a shape you inhabit, a red thread in the void.
